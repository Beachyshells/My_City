Plattsburgh’s always been my spot. I grew up here and love the small-town vibe, the Saranac River views, and the cool downtown scene. My go-to hangout is The Koffee Kat — the owners are the Kool Kats usually hanging out or working behind the counter, and it’s the perfect place to work or relax.

I started this website to share what makes Plattsburgh special. From the stunning Lake Champlain sunsets to the friendly community events, there’s so much to explore. Whether you’re into outdoor adventures, local eats, or just soaking in the small-town charm, Plattsburgh has something for everyone.
